The Requirements Engineering is a sub area of Software Engineering thatstudy the process of requirements definition that the software should support. The area born in 1993 with realization of the I International Symposium on Requirements Engineering. The process of requirements´s definition are an interface between clients wishes and necessities and a posterior implementation of this riquirements in software form.



RE Goals

Understand the necessities and support the clients wishes always was put as one of the biggests challenges of Software Engineering. The purpose of Requirements Engineering is to sustain methods, techniches and tools to Software Engineers that help the process of compreension and register of requirements that the software should attain. Diferent from others sub areas of Software Engineering, the requirements area have to deal with interdisciplinary knowledgement envolve, many times, social science aspects and cognitive science.



Who use?

Informatic professional and their clients when envolved in a process of requirements definition.
