Call for Papers

 

WER’01 invites submissions of high quality papers describing

 novel research  and/or experiences in the field of Requirements

 Engineering.

 

Papers presenting original results on both research as well as industrial

viewpoints are welcome on all aspects of Requirements Engineering.

 

The topics indicated are not restrictive, on the contrary they are listed as

a guide for the reader.

 

The articles will be reviewed and selected based on their originality,

significance and soundness.

 

Those articles having, in reviewers opinion, minor or formal defects,

whose content is relevant, will receive indication for their correction and

will be reviewed a second time, (see important dates).

 

Articles should be submitted through e-mail in Word, RTF or PDF

format.

 

The papers should not exceed 6000 words including figures, authors

information, abstract, and bibliography.  Papers may be in English,

Portuguese or Spanish. It is strongly recommended the use of English

for presentation graphic materials and participants should be prepared

to discuss their papers in English.
