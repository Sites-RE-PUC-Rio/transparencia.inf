Workshop on Requirements Engineering 2001

 

The fourth Workshop on Requirements Engineering will be carried out

at Facultad Regional Buenos Aires, Universidad Tecnológica Nacional,

on November 22nd and 23rd.

 

This workshop has alternatively been held since 1998 in both countries,

Brazil and Argentina, being its main aim the consolidation of an

Ibero-American research community on Requirements Engineering.

 

WER 98 was held in Maringá, Brazil, in October 1998.

 

WER 99 was held in the Facultad de Ingeniería, Universidad de Buenos Aires,

as a joint event of the 28th JAIIO, in September 1999.

 

WER 00 was held in the Pontificia Universidad do Rio de Janeiro in July 2000.

 

The articles presented at the Workshops are available at workshop home page.
